Why LAQAB

A premium brand on top of real production capacity.

Most embroidery sellers are local, fragmented and offline. LAQAB packages the same craft into an investor-grade brand: curated design, WhatsApp conversion, artisan coordination and repeatable fulfillment.

The opportunity

Indian weddings are visual, emotional and status-driven. Groomwear is under-branded compared to bridalwear. LAQAB focuses on the groom and family ceremony market where customization, trust and speed matter.

  • Premium sherwanis, bandhgala jackets, tuxedos and Indo-western sets.
  • India-first production with diaspora-facing sales.
  • WhatsApp as the storefront, CRM and consultation layer.
